Our Story

Family-owned, community-rooted, flavour-obsessed.

Where Every Dish Tells a Story

Little Lemon was born from a simple idea: bring the honest, sun-drenched flavours of the Mediterranean to our neighbourhood. Founded in 2018, our restaurant started as a tiny 20-seat café and grew, plate by plate, into the place you know today.

We source ingredients from local farms whenever the season allows — because a dish is only as good as what goes into it. Our chefs train in the Mediterranean tradition: slow cooking, bold herbs, and the patience to let good ingredients speak for themselves.

Whether you are here for a quiet weekday lunch or a big weekend dinner with family, we hope you leave feeling as though you have been welcomed into our home — because really, you have.
